    F.A.Q

    • What is a hackathon?

      A hackathon is a 36-hour event where students come together to create a project from scratch. It is a great opportunity to learn new skills, meet new people, and have fun!

    • Is Cipher Craft free to attend? Are there travel reimbursements?

      Cipher Craft is completely free for all participants. We will provide food, drinks, and swag for all participants. We are not able to provide travel reimbursements at this time.

    • Am I eligible to participate in Cipher Craft?

      Students of all skill levels are welcome to participate in Cipher Carft. We encourage students to bring their friends and teammates to the event!

    • I’m not that good at coding, can I join Cipher Craft?

      Absolutely! We encourage students of all skill levels to participate in Cipher Craft. We will have mentors available to help you with your projects and answer any questions you may have.

    • What should I bring?

      All you need to bring is your laptop, charger, and a valid student ID/CNIC. We will provide food, drinks, and swag for all participants.

    • What is the team size limit?

      You can be on teams of up to 4. We will have a team building activity right after opening ceremony if you need to find team members!

    • When can we start working on our project? Can I work on a previous project?

      You cannot start until after opening ceremony. You may come up with ideas, but are not allowed to start coding. You cannot work on a previous project, but can use frameworks if you clearly credit them in your readme and differentiate what you made vs what you used.

    • What kind of activities will there be?

      We will post the schedule closer to the event. There will be workshops and activities to take a break and meet other hackers and our wonderful sponsors.

    • Will hardware be available?

      We do not have hardware available, but you are welcome to bring your own. Due to building fire codes, please do not bring soldering kits.

    • How do I sign up to be a mentor?

      You can sign up here.

    • Are you sending out acceptances? Is there a deadline to apply? Is there a waitlist?

      We will send out acceptances 5 days before the event. If you need to know before then to book travel, please reach out to our team for an early review. Applications will be closing once we reach the maximum amount of hackers we can support, but we will have a waitlist the day of the event for any local hackers who want to fill the spots of any accepted hackers who do not end up attending.

    • Do I have to be 18?

      Attendees must be at least 15 years old due to child privacy laws. If under 18, you can attend, but will need to fill out a liability form from the university.

    • Do I have to stay overnight?

      No, you can leave and come back.

    • I have more questions, how can I reach out?

      Drop your query to us at our Email. We'd be happy to answer any questions!
